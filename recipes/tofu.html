Soy Tofu from scratch

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Soy beans
  • 1 liter of water
  • From 1 to 3 tablespoons of vinegar (depending on the consistency you want to reach)

Preparation

  1. The night before, rinse, select and clean the beans.
  2. Cover the beans with two cups of water and leave them soaking overnight.
  3. The following morning, remove the water.
  4. Blend the beans with three cups of water.
  5. Pass the beans through a coriander. The liquid can be used to make soy beverage or tofu, the residue is called okara and has multiple uses.
  6. Do one or two more passes of the milk through the strainer.
  7. Put the milk on the stove at medium heat being careful to mix it occassionally.
  8. After it boils, leave it 20 more minutes.
  9. Remove from the heat.
  10. Let it seat for 20 minutes; then add from one to three tablespoons of vinegar or lemon juice.
  11. Put in a cheesecloth, forming like a ball and put a weight over so that the extra liquid goes out of the tofu.
  12. Let it seat overnight.
