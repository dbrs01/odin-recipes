Banana bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 2 medium ripe bananas
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil (or olive oil, or blended peanuts)
  • 1/4 cup of ground flaxseed
  • Optional: nuts, raisins, other fruits

Preparation

  1. Smash the bananas a little bit.
  2. Mix all ingredients.
  3. Bake at 300 Farenheit degree for 30 minutes.
  4. Do the knife test
  5. Let it seat overnight before eating.
