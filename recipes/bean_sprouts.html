Bean sprouts from scratch

Ingredients

  • A medium sized glass container
  • Mung beans enough to cover the bottom surface of the glass
  • Water to cover the beans

Preparation

  1. Put the beans in the jar and cover with water. Let it seat over night (or for 12 hours).
  2. The next day, use a strainer to drain the water.
  3. Rinse the beans and remove the water.
  4. Repeat the previous step until the beans germinate and have small green leaves.
